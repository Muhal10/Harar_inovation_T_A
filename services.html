Services

The Harar Innovation and Technology Agency (HinTA) offers a range of services to support technology-driven innovation and entrepreneurship in the Harar region of Ethiopia. Here are the key services provided by HinTA

  1. Home
  2. Services

Incubation Program

HInTA operates an innovation hub that provides workspace, mentorship, and access to resources for early-stage startups. The incubation program offers training on business planning, product development, marketing, and fundraising. Startups accepted into the program receive guidance from experienced entrepreneurs and industry experts.

Acceleration Services

For more mature startups, HInTA offers an acceleration program to help scale their businesses. This includes access to investment readiness support, pitch coaching, and connections to investors and venture capitalists. The accelerator also facilitates pilot projects, market access, and partnership opportunities for startups.

Funding Support

HInTA manages a $1 million seed fund to provide early-stage financing for promising startups. The agency also helps startups navigate the funding landscape and connect with other investors and grant providers. HInTA often co-invests with external investors to leverage additional capital for its portfolio companies.

Training and Workshops

The agency organizes regular training programs, workshops, and masterclasses on various entrepreneurial and technical topics. These cover areas like design thinking, coding, digital marketing, business model innovation, and more. The training sessions aim to upskill local talent and equip them with the necessary capabilities to launch and grow their ventures.
