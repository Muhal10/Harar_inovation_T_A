The Harari Regional State Innovation and Technology Agency stands at the forefront of technological advancement and creative problem-solving in Harar, Ethiopia. This agency is a catalyst for change, driving the region’s journey into the digital age.

    Incubation Program

    HITA operates an innovation hub that provides workspace, mentorship, and access to resources for early-stage startups. The incubation program offers training on business planning, product development, marketing, and fundraising. Startups accepted into the program receive guidance from experienced entrepreneurs and industry experts.

    Acceleration Services

    For more mature startups, HITA offers an acceleration program to help scale their businesses. This includes access to investment readiness support, pitch coaching, and connections to investors and venture capitalists. The accelerator also facilitates pilot projects, market access, and partnership opportunities for startups.

    Funding Support

    HITA manages a $1 million seed fund to provide early-stage financing for promising startups. The agency also helps startups navigate the funding landscape and connect with other investors and grant providers. HITA often co-invests with external investors to leverage additional capital for its portfolio companies.

    Training and Workshops

    The agency organizes regular training programs, workshops, and masterclasses on various entrepreneurial and technical topics. These cover areas like design thinking, coding, digital marketing, business model innovation, and more. The training sessions aim to upskill local talent and equip them with the necessary capabilities to launch and grow their ventures.

    Key Informatio about harari Regional State Inovation and Technology Agency

    The Harari Regional State Innovation and Technology Agency stands at the forefront of technological advancement and creative problem-solving in Harar, Ethiopia. This agency is a catalyst for change, driving the region’s journey into the digital age. It focuses on harnessing the power of technology to streamline public services, enhance educational systems, and support local businesses. By fostering innovation, the agency aims to transform Harar into a smart city, where technology is integrated seamlessly into daily life, preserving the city’s rich heritage while propelling it towards a future of efficiency and sustainability. Their work not only empowers the community through access to modern technology but also lays the groundwork for a thriving economy that balances tradition with innovation.

    • Focus areas for HITA include mobile apps, e-commerce, fintech, agritech, and renewable energy solutions that are relevant to the local economy and needs.
    • In 2022, HITA launched a $0.5 million seed fund to provide early-stage financing for promising startups emerging from its incubation programs.
    • The agency aims to position Harar as a hub for technology and innovation in the eastern Ethiopia region, helping to create jobs and economic opportunities for the local youth population.

    Features

    Here are the key features and capabilities of the Harari Innovation and Technology Agency (HITA) that make it a unique and impactful organization in the region.

    Innovation Hub:

    - HITA operates a dedicated innovation hub in the heart of Harar's city center.

    • - The hub provides co-working spaces, meeting rooms, and other facilities for startups and entrepreneurs.
    • - It serves as a central gathering place for the local tech community, fostering collaboration and networking.

    Incubation and Acceleration Programs

    The acceleration program focuses on scaling up more mature startups, offering investment readiness support and market access opportunities.

    The incubation program provides mentorship, training, and access to resources to help founders transform their ideas into viable businesses.

    Ecosystem Development

    HITA actively works to strengthen the technology and innovation ecosystem in the Harar region.

    • The agency collaborates with universities, government agencies, and industry partners to create a conducive environment for startups to thrive.
    • HITA hosts networking events, hackathons, and community-building initiatives to foster collaboration and knowledge sharing.

    Policy Advocacy

    HITA engages with policymakers and regulators to advocate for policies and regulations that support tech entrepreneurship and digital transformation.

    The agency provides advisory services and insights to help shape the region's digital development agenda.
