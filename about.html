The Harar Innovation and Technology Agency (HinTA) is an organization based in Harar, Ethiopia that focuses on promoting technological innovation and entrepreneurship in the Harar region. Some key information about HITA.

  • HinTA was established in 2019 with the goal of fostering a vibrant technology and startup ecosystem in Harar and the surrounding Oromia region.
  • The agency provides various programs and services to support local entrepreneurs and innovators, including incubation, acceleration, training, and access to funding.
  • HinTA runs an innovation hub in downtown Harar that offers co-working space, mentorship, and networking opportunities for startups and tech companies.

The agency has partnered with universities, government agencies, and international organizations to organize hackathons, workshops, and other events to spur new technology-based business ideas and projects. Focus areas for HITA include mobile apps, e-commerce, fintech, agritech, and renewable energy solutions that are relevant to the local economy and needs. In 2022, HITA launched a $1 million seed fund to provide early-stage financing for promising startups emerging from its incubation programs. The agency aims to position Harar as a hub for technology and innovation in the eastern Ethiopia region, helping to create jobs and economic opportunities for the local youth population.
